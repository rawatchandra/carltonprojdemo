Our Company

Established in 1992, Carlton Leisure (UK) Limited has a successful history of over 25 years as an Air Ticketing, Holiday Business & Tour Operator in London.

Over the last decade we have grown rapidly establishing ourselves as global leader in the travel business and we have ensured efficient and timely services to our valued customers from UK and beyond. Today together with our travel partners spreading across the globe, we deliver high quality travel services to all major global travel destinations in the world. Carlton Leisure has expanded its travel business operations into a multi office service endeavor with 4 offices in the Greater London. Our partnerships in the airline industry, link us with many International airlines online and offline to handle our Air Ticketing services quickly and efficiently to any destination in the world. We are built on principles and core values of customer satisfaction, trust, friendliness, and quality of service, value for money and accountability & continual improvement.
